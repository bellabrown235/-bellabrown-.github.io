Welcome to Cooking meets College!

Our focus is cooking off campus for college students. Our goal here is to provide easy step by step instructions on how to cook yummy meals.

We also offer ways for students to have meals delievered to their houses. We simply collect the ingredients, and then send them your way along with a list of step-by-step instructions on how to cook your meal!
